The pool

Predict, score, brag.

A clean four-tier scoring system. Tiebreak: total > exact results > correct outcomes.

5Points
Exact result
"Predicted 2-1 — final score 2-1."
3Points
Correct outcome
"Home win called, scoreline missed."
1Point
One team's goals correct
"Got Brazil's two — missed the rest."
0Points
Nothing correct
"Better luck on the next one."
